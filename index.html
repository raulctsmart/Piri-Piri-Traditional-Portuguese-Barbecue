About Us

Welcome to Piri-Piri, your gateway to the vibrant flavors of traditional Portuguese barbecue in the heart of Cape Town, South Africa. At Piri-Piri, we take pride in offering an authentic dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Portugal.
Our story begins with a passion for great food and a desire to share it with the world. Inspired by the lively streets of Lisbon and the coastal charm of Porto, we set out to recreate the warm and inviting ambiance of a traditional Portuguese barbeque right here in Cape Town.

The heart and soul of our menu is the famous Piri-Piri chicken. We meticulously prepare each chicken, marinating it in a secret blend of spices and herbs, and then grilling it to perfection over an open flame. The result is tender, juicy chicken with a delightful hint of smokiness and a fiery kick of Piri-Piri sauce. Our commitment to using the freshest locally sourced ingredients ensures that every bite is an explosion of flavor.

But Piri-Piri is more than just chicken. Our menu features a diverse array of Portuguese delights, from succulent grilled prawns to mouthwatering beef espetada. Each dish is crafted with care, combining traditional recipes with a modern twist to create a truly memorable dining experience.
